MINI PIZZA COOKIES

HOW TO MAKE MINI PIZZA COOKIES

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tube pizza dough
  • pizza sauce
  • pepperoni
  • shredded mozzarella

    •

    PROCEDURE

  • Preheat your oven to 400Â°F.
  • Roll out your dough and use a 2 inch round cookie cutter to cut out rounds. Top with sauce, shredded mozzarella and pepperoni.
  • Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, keeping an eye on the pizzas, until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden liown and crispy.
  • Remove from the oven, let cool, stuff your face and eat away your emotions.
  • Remove the lid, fluff, and enjoy!
