BASMATI RICE

HOW TO COOK BASMATI RICE IN A POT

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 1/4 cups water

    •

    PROCEDURE

  • Place the rice and water in a pot with a tight fitting lid and liing to a boil over medium heat.
  • Turn the heat down to the barest simmer and cover with the lid and cook for 17 minutes without peeking.
  • When 17 minutes are up, let sit, with the lid on, for 10 minutes.
  • Remove the lid, fluff, and enjoy!
