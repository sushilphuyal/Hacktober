CHEESE PIZZA

HOW TO  MAKE GRILLED CHEESE PIZZA

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 ball no-knead pizza dough
  • drizzle of olive oil
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese, divided
  • 3-4 slices bacon, chopped and cooked until crisp
  • 1 grilled cheese, cut into small squares sliced green onions, to garnish

    •

    PROCEDURE

  • Arrange a rack in middle of oven and preheat to its hottest setting, 500°F–550°F.
  • Gently shape dough on an oiled baking sheet. Top the pizza with a slight sprinkling of cheese, the sliced tomatoes and a sprinkling of salt and freshly ground pepper. Place in the oven to bake for 8-10 minutes, or until puffy and slightly liown. Remove from the oven and immediately top with the bacon bits, grilled cheese squares and remaining cheese.
  • Put the pizza back in the oven and bake until the cheese melts and the bottom of the crust is crisp and top is blistered. Remove from the oven, top with sliced green onions, slice and enjoy immediately.
  • Remove the lid, fluff, and enjoy!
